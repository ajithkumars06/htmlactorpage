vijay

Vijay was born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, to S. A. Chandrasekhar, a film director, and Shobha Chandrasekhar, a playback singer. He has a younger sister, Vidhya, who tragically passed away at the age of two. Vijay attended Fathima Matriculation Higher Secondary School and later Balalok Matriculation Higher Secondary School. He pursued a degree in visual communication at Loyola College but dropped out to focus on his acting career.
